When Clicked

The When Clicked​ prompt is: ​Create a project where a user gets a surprise whenever they click on the stage or a sprite.

In this module, students explore interactivity through the creation of surprises. Interactivity is the communication between a human and a software program. By creating a surprise, students explore how to cause something to happen whenever the user clicks the stage or a sprite.

This module is available as Google Slides. With the adjacent buttons, you can preview the module, edit the module by making a copy of the slides to remix and adapt for your teaching context, or download the module as a PDF.
